Iphone 7

The Iphone 7 was released on September 16, 2016. This phopne came out with many different colors, such as rose gold, gold, silver, black, and jet black. It can hold double the storage the Iphone 6 held, which is 256 gigabytes. The Iphone 7 has a 4.7 inch to display the multi-touch screen. The size of the Iphone 7 is 5.44 inches tall, 2.64 inches wide, .28 inches deep, and weighs 4.87 ounces. Compared to the older models of the Iphone, this is the first Iphone that is water resistant. The camera quality captures moments of the world in 4k pixelation. A new addition to the sensors is a barometer, which measures airpressure and gives the user detailed information about the environment. The Iphone 7 also comes with built in apps such as the camera app, podcasts, reminders, wallet, etc. These apps are permantely in the phone and cannot be deleted by the user. The IOS limit on the Iphone 7 is 12, which allows for a plethra of features and new functions for Siri. AN interesting feature apple added to the Iphone 7 was jokes and other quirky interactions the user can have with siri. Apple gives an apple called Imovie for free, which allows the suer to be able to make cool videos with a free editing platform. The Iphone 7 offers bilingual language features for users who speak languages such as arabic, chinese, dutch, portuguese, and Hindi.
