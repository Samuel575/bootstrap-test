Iphone 6

The Iphone 6 was released on September 19, 2014. Apple likes to release newer versions of their phones around the same time each year. However, they capitalize on their products by making small consistent improvements instead of releasing an overall better product. The Iphone 6 Holds double of the capacity that the Iphone 4 holds, which is 128 gigabytes. It comes in 3 different finished colors:silver, gold, and space gray. As a user of the Iphone 6 , I believe this is one of the best Iphones released in the earlier days.It's battery life lasts for 10 days, which is a significant change to the older models. A new feature the Iphone 6 has is called "apple pay" which allows the user to have money on the mobile device.
